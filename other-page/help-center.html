Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
Log in to your account → Go to "My Orders" → Click "Track Package". You’ll also receive a tracking link via email and SMS.
What is your return policy?
We offer 30-day free returns. Items must be unused with original tags. Simply start a return from your account.
How long does shipping take in Pakistan?
Lahore/Karachi/Islamabad: 1-3 days
Other cities: 3-7 days
Free shipping on orders above PKR 3000
What payment methods do you accept?
JazzCash, Easypaisa, Bank Transfer, Credit/Debit Card, Cash on Delivery (COD) available across Pakistan.
